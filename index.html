About Jermaine

Jermaine the owner, is a North Shore native with over 20 years experience as a barber and men’s hair stylist. His credentials include extensive hands-on styling techniques including a decade traveling throughout the country performing platform & educational work as an American Crew All-Star. Jermaine has competed-in and won numerous professional competition awards and is consistently ranked among the top stylist in his profession. Jermaine still travels nationally to keep up on the current trends in men’s hairstyles, but now concentrates more of his time training & perfecting the skills of his own staff. No matter who serves you at Montrell's, rest assured they have been and continue to be personally mentored by Jermaine.
